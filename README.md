# 📊 Clustering of Districts/Cities Based on 8 National Education Standards (SNP)
This project analyzes the clustering of districts/cities in Banten Province and DKI Jakarta based on the average scores of the 8 National Education Standards (SNP) in 2018 using hierarchical clustering (Average and Centroid Linkage). The objective is to identify regional groupings based on education quality performance. The analysis found that the optimal number of clusters is two, where regions in DKI Jakarta generally have higher aggregate SNP scores compared to most regions in Banten.

## 🛠️ Tools & Methodologies
- R
- Hierarchical Clustering
   - Average Linkage
   - Centroid Linkage
